About Break Shit

Break Shit is a rage room in Tallinn, Estonia. Located within a walking distance from Telliskivi Creative Center, our rage room offers a perfect outlet for you to unleash your inner animal and go wild. Come to our rage room to smash stuff and have fun.

There are times when you feel down. King Solomon had a ring engraved with the words: “This too shall pass”. Don’t waste your time waiting, just come to our rage room, scream your lungs out, and thrash the crap out of the seductively fragile stuff we prepared for you. Have a blast and loosen up, and let’s break shit!

How does it work

You choose a package

Choose what you want to smash, date and time, and pay for your order.

We get everything ready

We will provide you with protective equipment and give you the shit to break – based on the package you selected.

Choose your destruction tools

We have hammers, golf clubs, baseball bats, and many more.

Break shit!

We won’t judge you.



Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I come with my partner?

    Absolutely! According to widespread data, breaking shit with your significant other releases oxytocin and reverses aging. Book your slot now and rage on!

  • My mom is turning 60 and I want to get her a gift voucher. Can she come?

    I don’t need to tell you that mothers are the most frustrated creatures on Earth. Even if she didn’t beat the crap out of you when you were a kid, believe me, she will enjoy the empowering sense of destruction! But honestly, we don’t care how young or how old you are. Bring your folks and ruin the whole place. Young people under the drinking age should come supervised.

  • What can I do in a rage room?

    We welcome rage within legal limits. Break phones, not bones. If you want to have some old-school fun breaking shit, come to our Break Shit rage room. Come alone or with friends.

  • What’s all that shit that I can break?

    Depending on the package, you will get a set of assorted smashables, furniture pieces, tearable plush toys. We will set a dinner table for you and let you do that tablecloth trick.

  • Can I get more shit to break?

    Of course! You can always get more shit onsite.

  • What kind of tools do you have?

    We have hammers, golf clubs, baseball bats, and many more.

  • What kind of protection do you have?

    We provide full protective gear including helmets, gloves, and protective glasses. It’s perfectly safe and you don’t need to worry.

  • I’m a peaceful person. Do I really need to break shit?

    I’m sure you have experienced the boiling rage that blistered and shifted under your skin at least once in your life. And if not, let me tell you, breaking shit is fun whether you do it alone, with friends, or your significant other. Loosen up and have a good time.

  • Can I get my money back if I’m still angry?

    I’m sorry to hear that. Book another time and we’ll get you more shit to break. If you get stressed on a regular basis, we can give you a discount.
